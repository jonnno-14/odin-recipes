Lasagne

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel (or ricotta) filling, and melted cheese. It's a comforting and hearty meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Prepare the noodles: Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the meat sauce: In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with chopped onion and garlic. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, and the egg. Season with the salt and pepper.
